Common questions

Is this safe?

Fair question. Here are the most common ones.

Are these platforms legal in Canada?
Yes. All platforms listed here are registered with FINTRAC (Canada's financial intelligence agency) and regulated by Canadian securities commissions. They operate legally in all provinces.
How much money do I need to start?
Most platforms let you start with as little as $20–$50 CAD. You don't need to buy a whole Bitcoin — you can buy a fraction, like $25 worth.
Why should I use your referral link?
When you sign up through my link, you get a welcome bonus you wouldn't get by going directly. I also get a small reward. It costs you nothing extra — the platforms pay for it as a marketing expense.
Do I have to pay taxes on crypto in Canada?
Yes. The CRA (Canada Revenue Agency) considers crypto a taxable asset. If you sell at a profit, you'll owe capital gains tax. Keep records of your transactions. When in doubt, consult a tax professional.
Which platform should I choose?
